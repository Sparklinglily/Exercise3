Introduction

This is description of the play,"Me and You". It illustrates the concept of individuality in conjunction with eternity of human existence. It is a well designed play with an African setting that portrayes the heritage of the Afrucan race.Feel at home going through all the scenes and revamp your understanding of the African Culture..

Description of Characters

There are four key Characters in the play. Bola,Aisha, Okon and Emeka. Bola is the wife of Okon who is from the Souther Region of Nigeria,West Africa. Emeka is the husband of Aisha from the Northern REGION oF Nigeria, while Emeka is from the Eeastern part of Nigeria#,West Africa. The key player of the scene is Aisha.
